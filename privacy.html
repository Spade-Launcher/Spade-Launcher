Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18.05.2026

Spade Launcher is designed with a privacy-first approach. Most app settings and preferences remain stored locally on your device.

Information Stored Locally

  • Favorite app selections
  • Hidden app selections
  • Gesture shortcut preferences
  • Theme and appearance settings
  • Weather city preferences
  • Wallpaper settings

Weather Functionality

If weather functionality is enabled, Spade Launcher may send:

  • City search text
  • Selected city coordinates

Weather information is provided by Open-Meteo. Open-Meteo may receive technical information such as IP addresses during requests.

Permissions Used

  • INTERNET
  • SET_WALLPAPER
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below)

Data Collection

Spade Launcher does not use:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising
  • User accounts
  • Crash reporting
  • Tracking technologies
