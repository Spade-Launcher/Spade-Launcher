Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26.05.2026

Spade Launcher is designed with a privacy-first approach. The app does not use analytics, advertising, user accounts, crash reporting, or tracking technologies.

Information Stored Locally

Spade Launcher stores settings locally on your device, including:

  • Favorite app selections
  • Hidden app selections
  • Gesture shortcut preferences
  • Theme and appearance settings
  • Weather city preferences
  • Wallpaper settings

Spade Launcher also reads the list of launchable apps installed on your device to provide the app drawer, favorites, hidden apps, and shortcut features. This app list is not sent to Spade Launcher servers.

Weather Functionality

When weather is used, Spade Launcher sends the following information to a self-hosted weather server operated by the developer (weather.spadelauncher.com):

  • City search text (sent to Open-Meteo's geocoding API to resolve city coordinates)
  • Selected city coordinates (sent to the self-hosted server to fetch the forecast)

Spade Launcher does not request precise device location permission for weather. Weather is based on the city selected by the user.

Weather forecasts are served from a self-hosted server using data from DWD and NOAA, processed with the open-source Open-Meteo software. City search uses Open-Meteo's public geocoding API. The self-hosted server may receive technical information such as IP addresses during requests.

Permissions Used

  • INTERNET: used for weather data and city search
  • SET_WALLPAPER: used to set wallpapers when requested by the user
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: used on Android 12 and below to select wallpaper images

Ace Edition Purchase Verification

When purchasing or restoring Ace Edition, the app sends a cryptographic purchase receipt (issued by Google Play) to a self-hosted server operated by the developer (api.spadelauncher.com) to verify the purchase is genuine. No personal data is included — only the signed receipt from Google. The server does not store this data after verification.

Data Collection

Spade Launcher does not collect, sell, or share personal data for advertising or tracking.

Spade Launcher does not use:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising
  • User accounts
  • Crash reporting
  • Tracking technologies

Data Deletion

Most data used by Spade Launcher is stored locally on your device. You can remove this data by changing settings inside the app, clearing app data, or uninstalling Spade Launcher.
