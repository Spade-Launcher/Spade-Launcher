Weather data

Weather forecasts are sourced from DWD (Deutscher Wetterdienst) via the ICON-EU model for European locations, and from NOAA/NCEP via the GFS model for global coverage. City search and geocoding are provided by Open-Meteo.

Weather data is processed and served using the open-source Open-Meteo software (AGPL-3.0). DWD ICON data is made available under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License . NOAA GFS data is provided as a US government work and is in the public domain.
