About

Built with
purpose.

As an aerospace engineering student, I rely on being able to focus. Spade Launcher began as a passion project to make my home screen less cluttered and less stimulating, so my phone could feel more like a tool and less like something constantly asking for my attention.

Every feature earns its place by answering one simple question: does it make the phone more useful without making it more distracting?

FAQ

Does Spade Launcher collect my data? +

No personal data is collected. When purchasing Ace Edition, a cryptographic receipt issued by Google Play is sent to a self-hosted server to verify the purchase — no personal information is included. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I switch back to my old launcher? +

Yes, anytime. Just change your default home app in Android Settings. No hoops.

What is Ace Edition? +

It's the premium version of the launcher with more customization, widgets, Themes, App Icons available, as well as the possibility to add your own icon packs and so on.

Are there ads? +

No. Never will be.
