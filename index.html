The idea behind Spade

Spade Launcher keeps the tools that make your phone useful while removing the visual noise that makes it distracting.

FAQ

Good to know.

Does Spade Launcher collect personal data? +

Spade does not use advertising or analytics and stores launcher preferences locally on your device. Weather requests are handled through Open Meteo.

Can I return to my old launcher? +

Yes. You can change your default home app at any time through Android settings.

What is Ace Edition? +

Ace Edition is the premium version of Spade Launcher. It includes additional customization, widgets, and advanced features.

Does Spade contain advertisements? +

No. Spade Launcher does not include advertisements.
