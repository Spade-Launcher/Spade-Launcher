The idea behind Spade

“Minimalism should remove friction, not functionality.”

Spade Launcher is not about turning your phone into an empty screen. It is about keeping what is useful, removing what is distracting, and making every interaction feel deliberate.

Independent project

Built with intention.

Spade Launcher is independently designed and developed by Nikitas Amvrosov with a focus on thoughtful interaction, privacy, and visual simplicity.

Every feature is judged by one question: does it make the phone more useful without making it more distracting?

FAQ

Good to know.

Does Spade Launcher collect personal data?

Spade does not use advertising or analytics and stores launcher preferences locally on your device. Weather requests are handled through Open-Meteo.

Can I return to my old launcher?

Yes. You can switch your default home app at any time through Android settings.

What is Ace Edition?

Ace Edition is the premium version of Spade Launcher, offering additional customization, widgets, and advanced features.

Does Spade contain advertisements?

No. Spade Launcher does not include ads.
